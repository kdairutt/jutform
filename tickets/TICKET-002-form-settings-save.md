# TICKET-002: Form settings disappear on complex forms

**Priority:** High
**Reported by:** Customer (username: `poweruser`, form: 10) via Support

> "I spent yesterday afternoon customizing the notification email template on our big registration form — a proper branded HTML email with our team signature and a link to reach support. Saved it, logged out, came back this morning and the template is just chopped off mid-sentence. The last tag isn't even closed. Our simpler contact form has a one-line template and that one's been fine for months. Please fix — the half-email is going out to every new signup."

Other users with large forms have reported similar symptoms — settings on bigger forms silently come back different (or truncated) after a save, while settings on smaller forms are unaffected.
