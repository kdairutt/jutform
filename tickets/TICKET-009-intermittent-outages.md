# TICKET-009: Site becomes unresponsive during peak hours

**Priority:** Critical
**Reported by:** Multiple teams and customers

> "The whole site just freezes sometimes during the day. Pages won't load, we get timeout errors, and then after a minute or so everything comes back. It's been happening a few times a day during business hours. Nights and weekends are fine."

Operations has confirmed the issue affects all parts of the application — even simple pages time out when it happens. The outages are brief but recurring.
